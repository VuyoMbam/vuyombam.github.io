More about Vuyo

Vuyo Mbam is a South African energy sector analyst and advisor currently working at the Council for Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR), wherein he works in the Energy Centre under the Energy Industry research group whose focus is to support the country’s just energy transition and green industrial development. He has worked on a variety of projects that fit within the broad ambit of these two key areas of national interest, such projects include, inter alia, a Clean Cooking Roadmap that was developed for Mpumalanga province; an Offshore Wind Roadmap for South Africa; an analysis of the potential opportunities for localisation in the Solar PV and Battery Energy Storage Value Chains; and an assessment of the readiness of South Africa’s education and training ecosystem for the envisaged green hydrogen economy.
